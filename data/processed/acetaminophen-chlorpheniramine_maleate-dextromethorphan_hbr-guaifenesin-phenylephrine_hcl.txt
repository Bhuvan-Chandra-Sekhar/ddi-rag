=== Drug Interactions ===
Drug Interaction Precautions Do not use if you are now taking a prescription monoamine oxidase inhibitor (MAOI). (certain drugs for depression, psychiatric or emotional conditions, or Parkinson`s disease), or 2 weeks after stopping the MAOI drug. If you are uncertain whether your presciption drug contains an MAOI, consult a health professional before taking this product
