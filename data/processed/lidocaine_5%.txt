Drug: lidocaine_5%

=== Drug Interactions ===
Drug Interactions Antiarrhythmic Drugs - Lidocan™ IV should be used with caution in patients receiving Class I antiarrhythmic drugs (such as tocainide and mexiletine) since the toxic effects are additive and potentially synergistic. Local Anesthetics - When Lidocan™ IV is used concomitantly with other products containing local anesthetic agents, the amount absorbed from all formulations must be considered.
