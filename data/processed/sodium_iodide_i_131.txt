Drug: sodium_iodide_i_131

=== Drug Interactions ===
7 DRUG INTERACTIONS Certain drugs and iodine-containing foods interfere with the accumulation of radioiodide by the thyroid. Review the patients history, current medications, and recent diagnostic tests prior to the administration of Sodium Iodide I-131 Capsules Diagnostic.
