Drug: icatibant

=== Drug Interactions ===
7 DRUG INTERACTIONS 7.1 ACE Inhibitors Icatibant is a bradykinin B2 receptor antagonist and thereby has the potential to have a pharmacodynamic interaction with ACE inhibitors where icatibant may attenuate the antihypertensive effect of ACE inhibitors. Clinical trials to date have excluded subjects taking ACE inhibitors.
